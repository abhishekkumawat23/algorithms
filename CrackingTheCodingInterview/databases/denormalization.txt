Question:
What is denormalization? Explain the pros and cons.

Answer:
1. Denormalization is a database optimization technique in which we add redundant data to one or more tables.
2. This helps us avoiding costly joins in a relational database.
3. Cons are:
   a. Updates and inserts are more expensive.
   b. Data may be inconsistent if update not done correctly.
   c. More storage required.
